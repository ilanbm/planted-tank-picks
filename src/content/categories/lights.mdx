## How we think about lights

Three tiers:

- **Low-tech (no CO2):** budget LEDs like the Hygger 957 — enough for Anubias, Java fern, Cryptocoryne.
- **Medium-tech:** Fluval Plant 3.0 or similar. Programmable, dimmable, enough PAR for most stem plants.
- **High-tech (CO2):** Chihiros WRGB II class. Under this, carpets don't carpet, reds don't redden.

Run lights 6–8 hours per day on a timer. A siesta mid-day is optional; the research on whether it helps is mixed.

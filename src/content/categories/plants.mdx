## Low-tech starter list

Java fern, Anubias nana, Cryptocoryne wendtii, Amazon sword, Vallisneria. All tolerate lower light and no CO₂.

## Medium-tech additions

Ludwigia repens, Rotala rotundifolia, dwarf sagittaria, pearl weed. These want better light but tolerate low CO₂.

## High-tech only

Carpeting plants (Monte Carlo, HC Cuba, dwarf hairgrass), most reds, Buce. These need CO₂ to thrive.

## Active vs inert

**Active** substrates release nutrients and buffer pH down. Faster growth; replace every 2–3 years. Great for shrimp tanks with Caridina.

**Inert** substrates don't change chemistry. Dose fertilizers. Last forever. Great for community tanks with stable parameters.

Capping dirt or active substrate with sand is a classic low-cost setup if you don't mind a more involved install.

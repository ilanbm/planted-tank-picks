## Bettas and flow

Bettas are labyrinth fish that prefer calm water. Any filter you use, throttle the flow or diffuse it with a sponge or baffle.

A dual sponge filter is often the simplest answer: gentle by design, easy to clean, near-impossible to break. For a 10 gallon betta tank with a few plants, it's all you need. If you prefer an HOB, the AquaClear 50 on its lowest setting works — some people add a home-made baffle from a plastic bottle to further calm the output.

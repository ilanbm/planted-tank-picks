## Nano heater reality

Small tanks heat and cool fast. A heater with a preset dial you can actually read matters more than another 5% accuracy. Flat heaters like the Cobalt Neo-Therm are well-suited to nano tanks because they hide behind decor or plants instead of dominating a back wall.

For a shrimp-only 10 gallon that stays at room temperature, you may not need a heater at all — shrimp tolerate 68–76°F. For any tropical community setup, a heater is mandatory.

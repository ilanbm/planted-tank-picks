## Lighting a 20 gallon

The 20 gallon long's width is easy to cover. Most plant LEDs in the 24-inch class fit. Pick spectrum first, PAR second, features third.

For low-tech plants (Anubias, Java fern, Cryptocoryne), any decent LED works — a Hygger 957 is genuinely good enough. For medium-tech (stem plants, carpets without CO₂), step up to a Fluval Plant 3.0 for programmability and even light distribution. For high-tech (CO₂-injected, demanding species), the Chihiros WRGB II earns its price with color rendering that makes reds actually read as red.
